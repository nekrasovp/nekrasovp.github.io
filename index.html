The unusual advantage

Engineering informed by operations.

Before becoming a software engineer, I owned and operated businesses in logistics, supply, and trade — including complex deliveries to Russia's Far North.

That background changed how I see software. Architecture is an operating model. Reliability is a business property. Ownership, handoffs, lead time, and constraints matter as much in a codebase as they do in a supply chain.

I also hold an MBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management from HSE University.
